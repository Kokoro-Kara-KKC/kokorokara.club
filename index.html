Kokoro Kara (KKC) / Whitney HS Japanese National Honors (JNHS) Society Chapter was originally founded in 1987. Deeply intertwined with the Japanese language program on-campus, KKC/JNHS strives to promote excellence in students taking Japanese as a foregin language while enriching this experience with authentic connections to Japanese culture and activities.
